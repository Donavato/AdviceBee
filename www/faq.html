FAQ

  • Can I talk with a professional advisor through AdviceBee?

    Currently there is no personal chat functionality or professional services offered through AdviceBee, although these are features that will be added to future versions.

  • Can I trust advice given to my question?

    While advice may be monitored for inappropriate content, advice is subjective to the user and accuracy cannot be guaranteed. We encourage each user to take advice as only suggestions.

  • Can I change my email address?

    No. Unfortunately email addresses cannot be changed. Please create another account if you wish to use a new email address.

  • I want to be part of a group but can't find one that I like.

    Groups are made by the user of AdviceBee. Feel free to visit the Create a Group page to create your own group about any topic or subject that you would like.

  • I can't find the topic I want. Can I make a new one?

    Unfortunately, users cannot create topics at this time.

  • I want to get opinions about a painting I made. Can I post a picture?

    Yes! When creating your question, you have the choice to either take a photo or upload a photo from your phones gallery.
